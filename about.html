Our story

Built by users,
for users.

Arachnid started the way most good tools do — out of frustration with everything else on the market. We wanted a bot that didn't nickel-and-dime servers for basic moderation, didn't bury useful features behind a paywall, and didn't fall over the moment a server actually started using it.

So we built one. What began as a moderation utility for a single community grew into a full platform: music, economy, leveling, ticketing, and a visual server builder, all running on infrastructure maintained by volunteers who use the bot themselves every day.

We're not a company in the traditional sense — no investors, no ad revenue, no upsells. Just a team spread across moderation, support and development, all working toward the same thing: a bot that stays fast, stays free, and stays out of your way.

We build the features we'd actually want in our own servers — nothing more, nothing less.

— The Arachnid Team

What we believe

Our values.
Not just words.

Free, always
Core features stay free forever. We don't gate moderation or safety tools behind a paywall.
Privacy by default
We collect only what's needed to run the bot, never sell data, and stay transparent about what's stored.
Community first
Every roadmap decision is shaped by feedback from the servers actually using Arachnid daily.
Built to last
High uptime isn't a marketing line — it's an engineering standard we hold ourselves to.
Open about mistakes
Incidents get a plain explanation, not a press release. We'd rather earn trust than manage optics.
Run by real people
No faceless corporation — every staff member is a volunteer who's a member of the community too.
